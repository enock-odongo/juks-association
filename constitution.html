Our Constitution

The JUKS Class of 2021 Constitution outlines our governance and operational principles.

Preamble

We, the alumni of Kameji High School Class of 2021, establish this constitution to guide our efforts in promoting unity and development.

Article 1: Name and Purpose

The association, JUKS Class of 2021, aims to foster community development and alumni collaboration.

Article 2: Membership

Membership is open to all 2021 Kameji High School graduates who align with our values.

Article 3: Leadership

Leadership comprises a Chairperson, Secretary, and Treasurer, elected annually.

Article 4: Meetings

Quarterly meetings and an annual general meeting will be held to review progress.

Article 5: Amendments

Amendments require a two-thirds majority vote at a general meeting.
