Our Leaders

Meet the visionary leaders driving JUKS Class of 2021 forward.

Reagan Okoth

Chairperson

Reagan Okoth leads JUKS, overseeing strategic direction, chairing meetings, representing the association externally, fostering unity and driving community development initiatives to ensure alignment with our vision and goals for sustainable impact in Kameji.

Enock Odongo

Secretary

Enock Odongo manages JUKS records, coordinates communications, organizes meetings, maintains membership databases and ensures effective documentation, supporting transparency and smooth operations to advance our mission of alumni collaboration and community growth.

Venah Boicha

Treasurer

Venah Boicha oversees JUKS finances, managing budgets, tracking contributions, ensuring financial transparency and supporting fundraising efforts to sustain community projects, scholarships and operational needs for the association’s growth and impact in Kameji.

About JUKS

JUKS, Kameji Class of 2021 is an alumni association focused on community development and youth empowerment in Kameji.
