About JUKS Class of 2021

JUKS Class of 2021, formed by Kameji High School alumni in Migori County, Kenya, is committed to creating a lasting impact through unity and development.

Our Vision

To be a premier alumni association driving sustainable development and youth empowerment in Kameji.

Our Mission

To unite alumni, promote team-work and provide opportunities for collaboration and development.

Our Objectives

  • Support bereavements through initiatives for Kameji students.
  • Drive community development projects in Kenya.
  • Facilitate networking among alumni.
  • Promote environmental sustainability.
  • Empower youth through leadership and skill-building programs.

Our Goals

  • Launch a scholarship fund by 2027.
  • Organize annual community service events.
  • Build a mentorship network for students.
  • Partner with local organizations for community growth.

About JUKS

JUKS, Kameji Class of 2021 is an alumni association focused on community development and youth empowerment in Kameji.
